Using this without live compass: the number above is your Qibla bearing measured clockwise from true north. Use any compass app or your phone's built-in compass to find true north, then turn until you're facing that many degrees clockwise from it.

If the live compass seems inaccurate: move your phone in a slow figure-8 motion a few times to recalibrate its magnetometer — this is a common fix and not specific to this app. Metal objects, magnets, and some phone cases can also throw off the reading.
