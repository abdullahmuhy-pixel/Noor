Dates shown use the Umm al-Qura civil calendar (the standard tabular calculation used across much of the Muslim world) and may fall a day earlier or later than your local mosque's determination, especially around Ramadan and the two Eids, since many communities confirm the start of these months by local moon sighting rather than calculation alone. Always confirm dates for fasting and Eid with your own local moon-sighting authority.
