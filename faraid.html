Fara'id Calculator

Islamic inheritance shares, calculated per the classical fixed-share system.

This tool covers the most common heir combinations (spouse, children, parents, siblings) under mainstream Sunni fiqh. It does not model grandparents/grandchildren as substitute heirs, multiple layers of extended agnatic relatives, waqf/wasiyyah interactions, or differences between madhabs on edge cases. For any real estate distribution, please have the result verified by a qualified scholar — this is a study/estimation aid, not a legal or religious ruling.
