Being upfront about what this can and can't do: this course will take you from zero to genuinely reading and constructing real Arabic sentences, with the same hearts/XP/streak structure that makes apps like Duolingo effective for building a daily habit. What it still can't fully replace: listening to native speakers and real conversation practice with correction — those matter for actual speaking fluency, and no static app, including this one, can substitute for them entirely.
